Banana Cream Pie

This banana cream pie is made with homemade pudding. It is one of the yummiest desserts on earth!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 pounds venison stew meat
  • 3 onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 3 cups water
  • 7 small potatoes, peeled
  • 1 pound carrots
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flower
  • 1/4 cup water

Directions

  1. Brown meat n a large heavy pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onions, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, bay leaf, oregano, salt and water.
  3. Simmer, covered, for 1 1/2 to 2 hours, or until meat is tender.
  4. Add potatoes and carrots, cook until tender.
  5. Combine flour and water, stir into stew.
  6. Remove bay leaf before serving.
